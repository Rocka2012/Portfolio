About

Hello, I'm Maikel! Thanks for taking a look at my portfolio! I'm a student Game Dev at Noorderpoort, where I work with Unity and C# to learn how to make games.

In my free time I like to work on cars, make mods for Car Mechanic Simulator and play guitar. In the future, I wish to create my own horror game and publish it on Steam.

Hamster Galaxy Quest

You are Hampt, born to become an astronaut. At least... That's what you think. In reality, you are just a hamster with a dream. A dream that might actually become a reality, now that you have a chance to escape. Escaping however, isn't going to be easy. Along the way you'll have to defeat several enemies and solve puzzles to make your way to your destination: NASA. The best part? You don't even know how to fly a space ship. Despite that, you are sure that you'll reach your goal of going to space. (unfinished)
